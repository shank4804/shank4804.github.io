Started a new podcast around tech, startups, venture capital

I recently started a new podcast in the Kannada language spoken in my home town of Bangalore, India with my friend at www.techsamskruthi.com

Here we mainly talk about 3 things in an episode:

  • Overview of recent happenings in all things tech.

  • We pick a new and upcoming startup which has potential to go big and talk about their business model, venture funding, future potential etc.

  • Discuss general trends in gaming / tv show / movies with a tech focus.

Apart from these, we plan on bringing in folks who started with a similar career background and now have achieved meaning things in their career and talk about how they got where they are and what general career tips they can offer for the rest of us.

Viewing it as a Product

I personally hope to do a sizable number of episodes to call this a side project with a significant weight in it. The learning output I expect from this is to understand the Business to Consumer space where I see this podcast as an actual product. Our content is the product and we take in listener’s feedback and iterate on the content thereby doing a sort of product management with marketing, finding the next best things to talk about, distribution and venture finance.

I will share my learnings and my experience as and when I find time. Please stay tuned!
